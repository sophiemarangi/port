Teaching Philosophy


I have wanted to be an elementary school teacher all throughout my childhood. I was inspired by the imaginative teachers I had growing up and want to be that teacher for my future students.

In order to maintain a controlled, welcoming, and diverse classroom, I would make sure to incorporate the following values in my teaching:

  • To engage students both physically and mentally. My seasoned writing skills will allow me to create tailored lessons based on my students. Engaging in play learning as a mentor allowed me to see the different ways students learn.

  • Ensure for active communication between the teacher and parents. Using my knowledge of Adobe Illustrator, Excel, and Google Slides, I will keep parents up to date on lessons and expectations will ensure their child is prepared to learn.

  • Allow students to be themselves. Using the skills I learned as a mentor will allow for structured lessons and ensuring students show their personalities through their work.
